# Customer Personas

The shared `ProductStrategyService` provides five complete demo profiles. Each profile includes financial inputs, goals, challenges, expected recommendations, readiness scores, and an expected mission plan.

## Young Family

- Goals: second child and larger home
- Challenges: childcare, high-interest debt, limited reserves
- Expected plan: debt payoff, transition fund, childcare and leave confirmation
- Key test: can FutureMe coordinate two missions that share the same blockers?

## High Income Professional

- Goals: work-optional status and equity diversification
- Challenges: concentrated employer stock, high fixed costs, bonus dependence
- Expected plan: concentration limit, automated diversification, retirement target
- Key test: can FutureMe prioritize risk even when cash flow appears strong?

## Pre-Retirement Couple

- Goals: retirement in five years and parent support
- Challenges: healthcare bridge, sequence risk, uncertain support costs
- Expected plan: healthcare reserve, near-term spending bucket, market stress test
- Key test: can FutureMe explain uncertainty without false precision?

## New Home Buyer

- Goal: first home within 18 months
- Challenges: post-close liquidity and incomplete ownership-cost assumptions
- Expected plan: clear card debt, preserve closing reserves, set an all-in housing ceiling
- Key test: can FutureMe distinguish readiness from mortgage approval?

## Single Parent

- Goals: emergency resilience and education funding
- Challenges: single-income risk, childcare, limited monthly surplus
- Expected plan: increase runway, stage debt payoff, protect retirement
- Key test: can FutureMe balance competing goals without judgmental language?

## Demo Use

Persona switching is available in Profile. It is an executive and product-testing tool; it does not replace authenticated customer profile selection.
