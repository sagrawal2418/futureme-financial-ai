# Demo Script

## Opening

“Most banking apps show where money went. FutureMe shows where the household is going.”

## Walkthrough

1. Open the dashboard and point to the Financial Health Score.
2. Review the top three items in This Week's Financial Checkup.
3. Open Financial GPS and explain the three monthly actions.
4. Show the five-year difference between current and improved trajectories.
5. Review Money Leaks and open the high-interest debt opportunity.
6. Compare goal readiness for buying a home and having another child.
7. Open Life Event Planner and select Welcome a new baby.
8. Review monthly impact, one-time range, risk impact, and preparation steps.
9. Launch the linked scenario and inspect its risk factors.
10. Compare Move to Austin with Stay in New Jersey.
11. Ask FutureMe, “What is my biggest money leak?”
12. Close by showing that Android, iOS, and web use the same shared output.

## Executive Close

FutureMe demonstrates a bank experience that moves from transaction history to proactive, explainable guidance while preserving deterministic financial logic and backend-only provider controls.
